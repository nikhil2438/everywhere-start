About Exwindoor

We Creating & make Quality Doors

Exwindoor strive to create eco-friendly uPVC/Aluminium doors and windows that work beautifully. Our commitment of creating high performance durable products has led to the creation of an extensive range of customized doors and windows.

We have state of the art machinery setup followed by premium quality profile sourcing, professional production, installation and after sales service. We have been continuously innovating to provide very best to our customers.

Our Legacy

Exwindoor is a brand of Nikunj Projects Pvt. Ltd. (NPPL) under which company runs its uPVC/Aluminium Door & Window division. NPPL started its journey in 2014 with base in New Delhi and slowly spreading to other regions of India. In short span of 7 years we have handed-over 600+ projects successfully and work closely with influencers & re-sellers throughout India.

Beyond Windows & Doors

Apart from window & door division, NPPL is an innovative platform for quality 'supply & apply' of elegant exterior products for residential, commercial, institutional and hospitality sector buildings. Our high quality product range includes:

  • Exterior Compact HPL
  • Extruded Exterior Louvre Cladding
  • Expanded Metal Mesh
  • Aluminium & S.S. Modular Railing
  • Customized Metal Façade

NPPL runs this façade division under the flagship brand name of Nexaa.
