Alongwith a lovely view sometimes you get the menace of bugs and mosquitoes ruining the ambience. To mitigate that we offer sleek and almost invisible mesh options as add ons.

Pleated Mesh provides very good ventilation and light to pass through, giving you a clear view. When pulled aside they take up very little space when stacked.
